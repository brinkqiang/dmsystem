### 自动换源命令(linux)

curl -L https://gitee.com/RubyMetric/chsrc/releases/download/pre/chsrc-x64-linux -o chsrc && chmod +x ./chsrc

### 自动换源命令(win)

curl -L https://gitee.com/RubyMetric/chsrc/releases/download/pre/chsrc-x64-windows.exe -o chsrc.exe
